Journey Through the Lone Star State

Austin, Texas

Austin, TX is one of the most beautiful cities that is known for being the "Live Music Capital of The World." In more recent time, has exploded within the past 5-7 years into a diverse, tech, and food topia for all residents and tourists that visit this city. UT Austin Longhorns, the Bats Under the Bridge, Formula 1, MotoGP and the outdoor adventures keep people coming back for more.

Food

Trill Taqueria

Trill Taqueria is a tiny little food truck that packs huge flavor into their unique tacos. They offer breakfast tacos for those who want to start the day off right and a variety of tacos for the lunchtime crowd.

    What to expect:

  1. Lamb & Egg
  2. Boar Chorizo
  3. Bean, Egg & Cheese
  4. Sweet Potato - Black Garlic, Greens

Ramen Tatsuya

Ramen Tatsuya is a stapel in the city of Austin as one of the best ramen spots of all time. All of their locations are fun, staff is friendly, and ramen flavor is unlike any other in the United States.

    Whats to expect:

  1. Amazing Chashu
  2. Pork Bone Broth or Miso Hot/Not
  3. Corn, Veggies, Ajitama, Menma, Etc.
  4. Egg

Coffee

Merit Coffee

Merit Coffee is one of many coffee shops that are a must if you are in town visiting or a local. It originated in San Antonio as "Local Coffee," but made it's way into several cities here in Texas. The culture and atmosphere in this shop is always a treat and you can count on an amazing Mocha Latte art.

    Coffee Recommended:

  1. Cortado
  2. Mocha Latte
  3. Cold Brew
  4. Americano
  5. Any of their in house roasted bags of beans. The Ethiopian is my personal favorite.

Greater Good Coffee Roasters

Greater Good Coffee Roasters have made a name for themselves in the coffee culture here in Austin. They originally started in Dripping Springs with their coffee shop/coffee roasting setup and have recently opened up a shop on the East Side. Their warm, well lit, and natual wood building interior is one that is relaxing to come to and they have a Training Lab where you can pay to have a professional Barista show you how to Roast, Pour Latte Art, and pull an espresso shot.

    Coffee Recommended:

  1. Cortado
  2. Hot or Iced Mochas
  3. Cold Brew
  4. Americano
  5. Any Teas they have on the menu, all are refreshing
